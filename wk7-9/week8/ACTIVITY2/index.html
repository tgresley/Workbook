My Useless Object
Whilst speculating about ways physical computing can interact with uis and the world around it through the subversion of sense, i came up with the useless object of an invert colour sensor.
Whilst we see the version of colours around us, this object senses those values through a photoresitor/colour sensor, and inverts them in their colour code, displaying this completely different colour through and RGB LED.

Whilst I might find this pretty useful, i like the stupidness of it, but also what it could reveal about how we view colour, leaving our senses of light values in the world more up to computing.
